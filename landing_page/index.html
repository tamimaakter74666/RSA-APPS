Troubleshooting & Safety

Google Play Protect displays security prompts for applications compiled outside standard Developer Console processes because they don't have automatic Google signatures. To proceed safely:

  1. On the warning panel, tap the small "More Details" arrow.
  2. Tap the bottom text option: "Install Anyway".
  3. The system wraps up setup and registers your portal flawlessly.
