### 🛠️ Troubleshooting & Safety

#### "What if the installation is blocked by Play Protect?"
*   **The Cause**: Google Play Protect occasionally flags third-party packages that are not registered through the Google Developer Console as potentially unrecognized.
*   **How to Bypass**:
    1.  When the alert box stating *"Blocked by Play Protect"* is shown, swipe or tap the tiny **"More Details"** drop-down arrow in the warning box.
    2.  Click on the safe-release option: **"Install Anyway"**.
    3.  The installation will continue securely and conclude in seconds.

#### "Is my device safe from malware?"
*   Yes, our packages are compiled directly from the secure GitHub main repository. They are entirely safe, contain zero trackers or intrusive ads, and are scanned for threat signatures prior to major releases.
